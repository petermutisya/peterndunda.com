About Me

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Public Health from Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology. With experience in epidemiology, health promotion, and community engagement, I lead projects that make a real impact. Currently, I work part-time as a Projects/Programme Officer at UNIREPROC CBO, where I manage health campaigns and advocate for maternal and child health rights.

Portfolio

Here are some of the public health projects I've been involved in:

  • Maternal and Child Health Campaigns - Led advocacy programs to support health education for mothers and children in underserved communities.
  • Waste Management & Infection Control - Implemented hygiene protocols and managed waste disposal in clinical settings to prevent infections.

  • Health Policy Advocacy - Engaged in advocacy for health policy reforms, focusing on improving health equity and access for vulnerable populations.
